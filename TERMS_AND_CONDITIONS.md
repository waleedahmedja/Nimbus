## 2. What Nimbus is

Nimbus is a weather information app. It displays forecasts sourced from Open-Meteo, a free third-party weather service, and location data resolved via Nominatim (OpenStreetMap). Weather data is provided for informational purposes only.

**Do not make safety-critical decisions based solely on information displayed in Nimbus.** If severe weather is possible, consult official meteorological services for your region.

## 3. Accuracy

Weather forecasts are inherently uncertain. Nimbus makes no guarantee that forecast data is accurate, complete, or current. Accuracy depends on Open-Meteo's data quality, which varies by region and time. The European AQI data displayed in the app is derived from Open-Meteo's air quality model and should not be used as a substitute for official air quality readings.
